Early Life

Aditya Gupta grew up in Mainpuri, Uttar Pradesh—a quiet soul with a mischievous streak and a mind full of curiosity. Though he wrestled with social anxiety and self-image, his early love for singing showed a spark of expression that later shifted to cricket and academics. Like many middle benchers, he coasted through school with quiet determination, never fully diving into extracurriculars but always absorbing the world around him. His time in Kota, meant for JEE prep, became a turning point—not through success, but through struggle. Depression pulled him back home, but it didn’t break him. Instead, it redirected him. At Galgotias University[2]Aditya found his true calling in tech and software development. What once felt like a setback became the foundation of his rise. Today, he stands tall in his belief: with hard work and consistency, he can achieve anything—and he’s proving it, one line of code at a time.
